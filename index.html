About Us

We are a Campaign that has a goal of encouraging not only our students to wear masks, but we also have a goal of encouraging the people of the world to wear masks. We want to show you what its like to be a teacher and student at Rivermont School - Chase City of New Story Schools. This is our way that we would like to encourage you to wear a mask and socially distance. We want to show you how hard its hard for our teachers, staff and students during COVID-19 from their side of the story.
